AI is transforming how we do science, run businesses, and govern societies. The changes ahead are larger and faster than most institutions are prepared for.

We help organizations and policymakers in the German-speaking world navigate this transformation. We combine deep technical understanding of AI with strategic and policy expertise to develop practical approaches for a rapidly changing landscape.

Who we are

Matthias Samwald, PhD, is Associate Professor at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence in Vienna. He co-chaired the Safety & Security chapter of the EU General-Purpose AI Code of Practice, working directly with frontier AI labs, regulators, and civil society. He leads the Accelerate Europe initiative on European AI strategy.

Jason Hoelscher-Obermaier, PhD, is Director of Research at Apart, an AI research organization, where he leads work on frontier AI evaluation and risk assessment. He contributes to the EU AI Office's work on evaluating manipulation risks under the EU AI Act. He also worked as an AI engineer at European startups. He holds a PhD in physics from the University of Vienna and a master's in philosophy of physics from Oxford.
